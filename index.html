About Me

Hi there! My name is Alex and I have an incredible passion for level, encounter, combat, and mechanic/system design, and love to create fun, interactive experiences. I am always looking to improve my skills as a designer and become the best I can be.

When I start a project is my aim is to improve and finish it with a high quality. I believe that every project, regardless of size, can be completed to a high standard and that a clear goal and good time management can see that come true.

My goal is to learn from the best in the industry and become a Creative Director. I want to lead an incredible team of people to make memorable and impactful games that players will remember for years.
